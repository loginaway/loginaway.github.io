Jiayang Cheng @ HKUST

Linkedin Google Scholar Semantic Scholar

I am a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) advised by professor Yangqiu Song, and a research intern at Amazon Web Services AI Lab with professor Zheng Zhang. Prior to my postgraduate studies, I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science from Fudan University.

My research interests are in Natural Language Processing, and I am particularly interested in knowledge grounding and analogical reasoning.
