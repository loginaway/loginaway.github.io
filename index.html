Linkedin Google Scholar Semantic Scholar

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Professor Yangqiu Song. I am currently working as an Applied Scientist Intern with the Amazon Rufus team in Palo Alto.

Previously, I was a research intern at Amazon Web Services AI Lab, working with Professor Zheng Zhang and Yue Zhang. I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science from Fudan University.

My research focuses on Natural Language Processing, with particular interests in knowledge grounding and analogical reasoning.
