I am a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) with professor Yangqiu Song. I am currently working as an intern with professor Zheng Zhang at Amazon Web Services AI Lab. Before moving to Hong Kong, I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science at Fudan University in 2021.

My research interest circles around knowledge grounding and analogical reasoning.
